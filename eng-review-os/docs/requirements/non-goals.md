# Non-goals (v1 / early MVPs)

Clarifies what **this product does not try to be** in early releases. Items marked **Later** may appear in Tier D (see [`phrase_detail_0.md`](phrase_detail_0.md)) after the core review slice is stable.

## Not in v1 (early MVPs)

- **Individual productivity scores** from Git/Jira/commits/PR counts as the **primary** performance signal.
- **Nine parallel personal “dashboards”** (one SPACE-style dashboard per engineer).
- **Full HRIS** or **deep two-way Jira** sync (read/write people, ratings, calibration bands).
- **Multi-tenant SaaS** and **compliance-heavy 360** workflows (unless your org mandates a bought tool instead).
- **Squad / team metrics** as stack-ranking or **individual comparison** from team dashboards (team *health* may be **Later**, designed carefully).
- **MBTI / personality** used as a **rating input** or automatic performance signal (optional profile field only **Later**, with privacy review—not a score).
- **1:1 tool replacement** for calendar, email, or official HR records (capture **notes** here only **Later**; v1 can stay performance-review focused).

## Explicitly in product intent (not “non-goals”)

- Evidence-based reviews: **narrative + links**; SPACE as **section headings**, not KPIs.
- **Fairness:** level expectations + documented examples—not leaderboards.
